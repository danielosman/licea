  *     *       *         * ## Masters and Robots 2025 Youth

        * Masters & Robots is a unique event where we explore the latest trends in technology, innovation, and leadership while connecting with inspiring people from around the world. This year's theme, "Time to act!", encouraged everyone to take meaningful steps towards shaping a better future.This year, we — students from classes 4b and 3b — had the chance to take part in workshops at Masters & Robots 2025 Youth in Warsaw. Accompanied by Ms. Godley and Ms. Drobek, we visited the TVN headquarters, where we watched a live broadcast seen by tens of thousands of viewers — and got to experience it all in person!The workshops focused on developing artificial intelligence, future careers in the job market, recognizing reliable information, and included developmental advice from experts. The lectures were both in Polish and English, adding an extra element of diversity and engagement.

Overall, we found this experience enriching, inspiring, and truly motivating.Events like Masters & Robots show how education connects with the world of innovation, leadership, and sustainability — helping us become confident, creative thinkers ready to shape the future.
