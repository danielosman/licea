## LXIV Liceum Ogólnokształcące im. St. I. Witkiewicza - Main Content

## Page Title
"Jesteśmy Szkołą Sukcesu!" (We Are a School of Success!)

## Key Rankings Announced

The school has achieved significant recognition in the 2025 General Polish High School Rankings:

- **13th place** nationally among general education high schools
- **6th place** in Mazovian Voivodeship rankings
- **6th place** among Warsaw high schools
- **17th place** in the National Ranking of Olympic Schools

The institution received the **"Golden School 2025"** designation.
