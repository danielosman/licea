# Szatnia (Cloakroom) - LXIV High School

## Main Content

The school has established a strict footwear policy: **all students are prohibited from wearing shoes indoors**. Shoe changes must occur in the designated cloakroom area, not in hallways or classrooms.

**First-year students** receive locker keys from room 104 after paying a 25 PLN deposit. This deposit is refunded upon graduation and key return.

## Additional School Information

The page also highlights:

- **Recent Achievement**: The school ranked 13th nationally in the 2025 General High School Rankings and 6th in a specialized category
- **Experimental School Status**: LXIV LO has operated as an experimental high school since 2019/2020
- **Olympic School Recognition**: Students regularly win competitions and olympiads, enhancing university admission prospects

The school is named after St. I. Witkiewicz and is located in Warsaw's Żoliborz district.
