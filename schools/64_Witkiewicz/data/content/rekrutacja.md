## Key Information

**School Overview:**
This is an experimental general secondary school in Warsaw named after Stanisław Ignacy Witkiewicz. The institution emphasizes a friendly atmosphere and has become increasingly popular among students in the Żoliborz district.

**Enrollment Plan:**
Six first-year classes are opening for the 2026/2027 academic year, all structured as experimental divisions featuring developmental tutoring.

**Class Organization by Foreign Languages:**
- Classes 1a–1f each offer different language combinations
- English appears as the extended (advanced) level in most classes
- Other extended-level options include: German, French, and Spanish
- Students choose whether to expand subject offerings after the second trimester of their second year

**Recruitment Timeline:**
Document submission window: July 17–22, 2025 (12:00 noon to 12:00 noon)
Final candidate lists published: July 22, 2025 (by 4:00 PM)

**Required Documentation:**
Qualified candidates must submit original certificates of completion, exam results documentation, and a passport-style photograph.

**School Achievements:**
The institution ranks 13th nationally in the 2025 General Secondary School Rankings and maintains status as an Olympic school with consistent student participation in competitions.
