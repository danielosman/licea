# References from LXIV High School

The webpage displays testimonials from current and former students about their experiences at the school. Here are the main references presented:

**Hubert Taładaj** describes the school as more than just an institution: "Witkacy" to nie tylko liceum, to po prostu życie, stan ducha..." (roughly: more than just a school, it's a lifestyle and exceptional atmosphere).

**Kinga Dziobiak** emphasizes the school's role in personal transformation, noting how the environment helped her develop both academically and socially, creating a sense of home within the educational setting.

**Piotr Bauer** highlights what he calls the school's "inność" (uniqueness/otherness), appreciating features like the absence of bells and the culture of self-development among students.

**Bartosz Bieliński** characterizes the institution as a place for self-realization and pursuing passions rather than merely an academic facility, allowing students to balance personal interests with exam preparation.

These testimonials collectively reflect student satisfaction with the school's distinctive approach to education and its supportive community environment.
