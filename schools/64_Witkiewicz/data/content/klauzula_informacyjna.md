# Klauzula informacyjna - LXIV Liceum Ogólnokształcące

## Main Content Summary

The page presents an information clause regarding data protection for a Polish secondary school (LXIV Liceum Ogólnokształcące im. St. I. Witkiewicza).

**Key Points:**

The school emphasizes commitment to safeguarding personal data in compliance with GDPR regulations effective since May 25, 2018. The institution states: "Nie wykorzystujemy Twoich danych osobowych w celach innych niż przewidziane przepisami prawa" (We do not use your personal data for purposes other than those provided by law).

**Data Protection Commitments:**
- Personal data is not shared or sold to third parties
- Data processing is limited to what is necessary
- Data security is maintained throughout the educational service period

**Contact Information:**
Parents can request clarification from the Data Protection Officer (Inspector Ochrony Danych) or contact the school's main contact point at 604-362-559.

The page includes a downloadable PDF titled "Zgoda na przetwarzanie danych i wizerunku i ofertę Liceum" (Consent for Data and Image Processing and School Offer) for formal agreements regarding data handling.

The school's full privacy policy documentation is available through their dedicated RODO section.
