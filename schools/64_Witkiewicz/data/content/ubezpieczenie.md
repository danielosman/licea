The page announces that **InterRisk TU S.A.** won the bidding process for voluntary accident insurance (NNW) for students in the 2024/2025 school year.

## Key Details:

**What is covered:** The insurance covers accidents with benefits including compensation for injuries, medical treatment costs, rehabilitation, and orthopedic equipment purchases.

**Who can purchase:** Children and youth attending educational institutions, plus children of school employees, up to age 18 (or 26 if still studying).

**Cost options:** Five premium tiers are available: 43, 57, 71, 93, or 174 zloty per year.

**How to buy:** Parents visit https://nnw24.pl/, select a premium level, enter personal and child information, choose their child's school from the dropdown, then proceed to payment.

**Important note:** "Ubezpieczenie NNW jest dobrowolne" (accident insurance is voluntary).

A downloadable information sheet (NNW-ulotka-A4.pdf) provides additional policy details.
