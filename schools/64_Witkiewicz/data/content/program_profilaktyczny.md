# Program wychowawczo-profilaktyczny w LXIV LO

The educational and preventive program at LXIV High School (Witkacy) outlines comprehensive goals and methods across multiple areas:

## Key Program Areas

**Social Education** involves students in school life organization and charitable activities, fostering social sensitivity and maturity.

**Etiquette and Social Behavior** includes teacher examples, seminars, and conferences to develop appropriate conduct in various situations.

**Environmental Protection** integrates ecological awareness through science classes, participation in environmental olympiads, and field activities.

**Protection from Negative Groups** uses discussions and expert presentations about group dynamics and associated risks.

**Sexual Education** features specialized seminars covering biological development, psychological aspects of puberty, and family planning.

**Emergency First Aid** provides training by qualified instructors for immediate response to injuries.

**Substance Abuse Prevention** delivers information through lectures, films, and student-led discussions to discourage psychoactive substance use.

**Physical Activity and Nutrition** emphasizes regular exercise and healthy eating habits through biology and PE instruction.

**Disease Prevention** addresses eating disorders, doping, and infectious diseases including AIDS, hepatitis, and metabolic conditions through educational materials and expert guidance.

Implementers include teachers, school psychologists, invited specialists, and parents working collectively to achieve these health and behavioral outcomes.
